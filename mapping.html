Key Elements of a Successful Map

Goals

Goals are what the user is hoping to accomplish. For a site like amazon, this might be to find and purchase a product. Overall, we want to give them the tools to do that, and if we can, we want to make it an enjoyable process.

Activities

Activities are what a user has to go through to accomplish their goals. In our Amazon example, this might be searching for a product by typing some values into a search box and browsing through a page of results.

Tasks

Tasks are the small steps a user takes in order to complete their activities. Clicking on buttons, filling out fields, scrolling through lists . . . every action, which we should account for and make as pleasant to use as possible.

Stories

Stories are what happens as a user completes tasks. When the user enters text into a search box, do we want it to auto complete? When they click a button, what kind of response do we provide? These are the fine details.
